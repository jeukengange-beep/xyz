Why this launch, why now?

  • Urban cores add +6% new renters yearly — kitchens are shrinking, delivery spend +28% YoY.
  • Current delivery players miss lunch SLAs 1-in-3 times during peak traffic.
  • Surveyed 212 residents: 64% would prepay weekly if time slots & diet choices were guaranteed.
Underline that we validated pain via secondary data and our own pulse survey last month.

Douala

400k targetable professionals within 10km of hubs.

Yaoundé

Competitive Playbook

Defend

Protect recurring seats from price wars.

  • Loyalty perks after 6 deliveries
  • Service recovery in <15 min with credit

Differentiate

Lean on predictability + wellbeing.

  • Chef bios + caloric transparency in-app
  • Guaranteed time slots & SMS ETA

Disrupt

Exploit rivals' operational gaps.

  • Pop-up tastings near their hotspots
  • Co-market with corporates for payroll deduction
